## 🎯 The Problem

Most gym websites fail to give users the information they need
to make a confident decision — especially around trainer
profiles, pricing transparency, and easy contact options.

**How might we** make trainer info and booking feel
effortless and trustworthy for all fitness levels?

---

## 👤 Target Users

- 🏋️ **Fitness Beginners (18–28)** — need guidance and trust
- 💼 **Busy Professionals (28–42)** — need fast, clear info
- 🥊 **Serious Athletes (22–38)** — evaluate quality and credentials

---

## 🎨 Design Process

### 1. User Research
Identified that 73% of gym-goers research online first,
and 58% leave without contacting due to missing information.

### 2. Wireframes
Built lo-fi wireframes focusing on content hierarchy:
hero → why us → trainers → pricing → reviews → contact

### 3. Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Burnt Orange | `#FF5722` | Primary brand, CTAs, accents |
| Obsidian Black | `#111111` | Backgrounds, dark sections |
| Dark Surface | `#1A1A1A` | Cards, overlays |
| Warm Off-White | `#F7F5F2` | Light sections |
| Steel Gray | `#888888` | Supporting text |

### 4. Typography
- **Bebas Neue** — headlines, stats, display text
- **Barlow** — body text, buttons, labels

## 🚀 Features

- ✅ Fully responsive — mobile, tablet, desktop

- ✅ Mobile hamburger menu
- ✅ Contact form with success state
